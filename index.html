How to Play

Controls

WASD - Move

Mouse - Look around

Left Click + Hold - Mine

Right Click - Place/use selected item

0-9 - Select hotbar item

E - Open inventory

F1 - Hide UI

Esc - Unlock cursor and toggle menus

Objective

Mine ores to collect resources, craft items, and dig as deep as possible.
